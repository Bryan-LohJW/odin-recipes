Chicken Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Pandan Leaf
  • Roasted Chicken
  • Braised Eggs
  1. Steam the white rice in a rice cooker with the pandan leaf
  2. Plate the rice and add the roasted chicken neatly beside
  3. Slice the braised eggs in halves and plate it
  4. Served best with Signature Chilli
