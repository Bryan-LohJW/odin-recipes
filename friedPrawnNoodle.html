Fried Prawn Noodle

Description

Ingredients

  • Yellow Noodles
  • White Thick Rice Vermicelli
  • Prawns
  • Chicken Stock
  1. Peel the prawns and cook them in a hot wok
  2. Add the prawn heads into the chicken stock and cook for 30 mins
  3. Add the prawns, chicken stock, and noodles and cook for 5 mins
  4. Serve it on a dried banana leaf
